About

Hello! My name is Sam Bakenhus, or you may also know me by my online handle SquidJelly. I'm a 2D Game Developer that's been working part-time since 2008, and full-time now since 2016.

I hold experience in nearly every aspect of game development working in many different genres and deploying for desktop, web & mobile. I'm comfortable in a wealth of different programming languages and libraries; Working with graphics, physics, logic, networking and AI. I pride myself on being a multi-disciplinary game developer, bringing whatever is needed to the project.

Games are still a relatively new form of media. I believe we'll continue to see them grow and dominate as an art form in the years to come and I'm very much looking forward to being a part of it.
